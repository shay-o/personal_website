Research Use Only. ⚠️ This system is for research purposes only. It evaluates AI-generated information quality but does NOT: - Provide medical, legal, or insurance advice - Replace professional Medicare counseling - Recommend specific health coverage decisions - Serve as a decision-support tool for beneficiaries Results should not be used to make healthcare decisions. This tool presents comparative evaluation data, not beneficiary guidance.
